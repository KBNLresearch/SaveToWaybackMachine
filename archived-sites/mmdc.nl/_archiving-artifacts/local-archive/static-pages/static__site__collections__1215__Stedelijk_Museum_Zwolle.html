How to reach us?

The walk from the train station Zwolle takes about 10 minutes. You can take the bus from the train station Zwolle: lines 2 or 3, bus stop: Rode Torenplein.

Number of manuscripts in the collection(s)

Two manuscripts, of which one is on permanent loan to the Duitse Orde in Utrecht.

Contact

Please address to muszwol@stedelijkmuseumzwolle.nl

Procedure for ordering manuscripts

Please write or call the head of the department several days before you plan to visit.

Other information about the collection

The Stedelijk Museum Zwolle is the result of a fusion between the Provinciaal Overijssels Museum and Librije Hedendaagse Kunst in 1996
