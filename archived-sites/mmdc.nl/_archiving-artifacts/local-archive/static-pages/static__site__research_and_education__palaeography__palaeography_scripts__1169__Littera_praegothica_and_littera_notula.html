Littera prae-gothica and littera notula

Littera prae-gothica is the transitional phase between caroline minuscule and littera textualis, occuring in the 11th and early 12th century.

In 'Medieval Manuscripts in Dutch Collections' littera notula indicates a script from a period wherein the opposition textualis-cursive is not yet firmly established. This was the case in the 13th century.
