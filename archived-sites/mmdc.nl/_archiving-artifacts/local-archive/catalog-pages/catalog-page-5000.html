    Lectionary

    Date
    1538, 1589
    Language
    Latin
    Material and composition
    Paper, 320 ff.

    .
    Contains binding fragment
    Dimensions
    279x198 (190x130) mm
    Layout and script
    23 lines, littera hybrida
    Binding
    16th-century binding
    Region of origin
    Maasland: Liège-Tongeren-Maastricht
    Place of origin
    Maastricht
    Provenance
    Two parts, dated 1538 (167r) and 1589 (286v). The first part was copied by frater Theodericus Zyttaert (van Sittard), Crutched friar in Maastricht
    Acquisition
    Confiscated c. 1794 by the French occupying forces in Limburg and rediscovered in Maastricht in 1839, acquired as part of the collection Maastricht
