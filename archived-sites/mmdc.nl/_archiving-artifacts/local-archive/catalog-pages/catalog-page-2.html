  • The Hague, KB : ms. 70 E 4

    Tongeren fragments / Usuard

    Author
    Usuard (-c. 875)
    Alternative title (Dutch)
    Tongerse fragmenten
    Alternative title (other)
    Fragmenta Tongrensia / Usuard
    Annotations on the text
    Contains: Readings for feastdays - Necrology of the Church of Our Lady, Tongeren - Martyrology / Usuard - Offices of Saints (SS. Anna, Dominicus, Mary and the childhood of Jesus, Francis) and several other liturgical texts
    .
    music notation
    Date
    13th-15th century
    Language
    Latin
    Material and composition
    Parchment, 127 ff.

    Dimensions
    Predominantly c.290x205 mm, written space varies
    Layout and script
    lay-out varies
    Binding
    16th-century binding
    .
    Brown leather binding from the monastery Ter Nood Gods, Tongeren, 1537
    Region of origin
    Maasland: Liège-Tongeren-Maastricht
    Place of origin
    Tongeren (et al.(?))
    Provenance
    According to a note at the inside of the front coverplate, this manuscript was repaired at the monastery Ter Nood Gods, Tongeren, in 1537. According to a note on f. 100v, owned by the Church of Our Lady, Tongeren
    Acquisition
    Confiscated c. 1794 by the French occupying forces in Limburg and rediscovered in Maastricht in 1839, acquired as part of the collection Maastricht
    Former owner and collections
    Church of Our Lady, Tongeren
    Collection Maastricht
