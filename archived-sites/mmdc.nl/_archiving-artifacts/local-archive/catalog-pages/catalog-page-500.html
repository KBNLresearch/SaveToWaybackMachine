  • The Hague, KB : ms. 77 L 60

    Book of hours

    Alternative title (Dutch)
    Penningenhandschrift
    Date
    c. 1440-1460
    Language
    Latin
    Material and composition
    Parchment, 98 ff.

    Dimensions
    196x134 mm
    Layout and script
    1 column, 21 lines, littera textualis
    Binding
    Post-medieval binding
    Illustration and decoration
    miniatures, decorated initials, pen-flourished initials, border decoration, silver and golden pilgrim badges sewn on f. 98
    Region of origin
    West Flanders
    Place of origin
    Bruges
    Acquisition
    By legacy, mainly in female line, to the families De la Chambre and De Montjutiez (c. 1467-c. 1500), De Bougne (c. 1512-1520, d''Orsan (c. 1537-1577), d''Oiselet (c. 1600-1633) and De Froissart, marquess De Broussia (c. 1650-19th cent.). Acquired in 1993 from antiquarian bookseller F. Knuf, Buren
