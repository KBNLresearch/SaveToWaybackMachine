- **[Two KB websites with medieval manuscripts archived in the Wayback Machine](Twee KB-websites met middeleeuwse handschriften opgenomen in de Wayback Machine, april 2026.md)** - *April 2026* - *Dutch only*<br/>
On December 15, 2025, two older KB websites containing medieval manuscripts were taken offline: manuscripts.kb.nl and mmdc.nl. To archive this digital heritage in a public and accessible way, we have placed them in the Internet Archive's Wayback Machine, totaling nearly 20,000 pages. The entire process was carried out with the help of an AI assistant. [Read the full story here](Twee KB-websites met middeleeuwse handschriften opgenomen in de Wayback Machine, april 2026.md).
